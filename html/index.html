Click on a field to see the rank of that field in comparison to all the other fields in the municipality of Stadskanaal, Netherlands.

A Score of 1 means: the best parcel.

A leaf-index of 1 means: highest possible vegetation amount.

A Score of 0 means: the worst parcel in terms of vegetation.

A leaf-index of 0 means: no living vegetation present.

The colouring of the fields indicate the ranks halfway the growing season.
